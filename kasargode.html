Kappil beach

Kappil beach, situated near the Bekal Fort in Kasaragod, is famous for its seclusion and rightly so. Among the finest and cleanest beaches in God's Own Country, this is a destination perfect for those who seek solitude and calmness.

Chandragiri fort

Chandragiri Fort is a fort built in the 17th century, situated in Kasaragod District of Kerala, South India. This large squarish fort is 150 feet above sea level and occupies an area of about seven acres adjacent to Chandragiri river.

Sea view park

The Sea View Park is to the end of the beach which is the only area with some tree shade. The park has ornamental plants, facilities for kids activity and a proper maintenance will bring more people in the evenings. Parking is available in front and on holiday evenings.
