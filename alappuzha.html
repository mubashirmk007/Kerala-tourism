Marari beach

Marari Beach, a secluded little known beach destination in the Alappuzha, is located about 11 km from Alappuzha town, Kerala in southwestern India.

Krishnapuram palace

The Krishnapuram Palace is a palace and museum located in Kayamkulam near Alappuzha in Alappuzha district, Kerala in southwestern India.

Kuttanad

Kuttanad is a region covering the Alappuzha, Kottayam and Pathanamthitta Districts, in the state of Kerala, India. Poovar Island is surrounded by a river called Neyyar.
