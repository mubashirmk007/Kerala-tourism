Eravikulam National park

Eravikulam National Park is a 97 km2 national park located along the Western Ghats in the Idukki and Ernakulam districts of Kerala in India..

Mattupetty Dam

Mattupetty dam is a concrete gravity dam and now a haven situated in Idukki district of Kerala for many wild animals and birds thanks to the constant supply of water.

Anamudi

Anamudi is a mountain located in Ernakulam district and Idukki district of the Indian state of Kerala. It is the highest peak in the Western Ghats and in South India.
