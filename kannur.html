Payyambalam beach

Payyambalam beach is a beautiful spot located in the district of Kannur. It is one among the five beaches that is collectively known as Kannur beach. Long stretch of sandy walkways and recreational facilities of this beach.

Kannur light house

Also known as Cannanore Lighthouse, the lighthouse is still active and overlooks the Arabian Sea. The British introduced a system of hoisting a lantern with an oil wick lamp in order to warn ships. A masonry pedestal was constructed on the rampart of Kannur fort.

St. Angelo fort

St. Angelo's Fort is a massive triangular laterite fort and is among the most historic sites in Kannur. Constructed by the first Portuguese Viceroy, Don Francesco de Almeida the fort is flanked by gigantic bastions that make for an imposing sight.
