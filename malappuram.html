Cheruppadi mala

Cheruppadi Mala and Mini Ooty viewpoints are two closely located picturesque sites of the Arimbra Hills, separated only by about 8 Km.

Kolayi view point

Kolayi is a beautiful destination in Malappuram district of Kerala. A view point and a water fall are the main attractions of this place.

Shanthitheeram park

Shanthi Theeram Park is a recreational park with a panoramic view of the beautiful natural forestation and water bodies. It is famous for adventure activities and water sports.
